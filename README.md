TMK Structural PlannerThe TMK Structural Planner is an interactive educational tool designed for teachers to navigate a specific, staged multiplication and division curriculum.🎯 The Core Philosophy: Products as ObjectsUnlike traditional methods that rely on memorizing a 10x10 grid, this app treats every number as a Product Hub—a central object that exists in a "Product World".The app follows the TMK Structural Law:Multiplication is the Entry: It represents the routes to a product (e.g., $4 \times 6 = 24$).Division is the Exit: It represents the routes away from a product (e.g., $24 \div 4 = 6$).🚧 The P10 BoundaryThis system operates strictly within the P10 World:A number is a "TMK Product" if and only if it has a factor pair where both factors are 10 or less.Example: 72 is included ($8 \times 9$).Example: 75 is excluded ($5 \times 15$) because 15 exceeds the P10 boundary.🗺️ Staged ProgressionThe app allows teachers to "unlock" the Product World in seven logical stages, moving from basic identity to complex doubling chains and final closure:Stage 0 & A: Entry and Identity (1–10).Stage B: Scaling by 10 (20–100 benchmarks).Stage C & D: Midpoints and Nine-structure logic.Stage E: The Doubling Chain ($2 \times \to 4 \times \to 8 \times$).Stage F: Interleaving and Compression (showing how families overlap).Stage G: Final Closure (the product 49).🛠️ How to Use This AppSelect a Stage in the sidebar to reveal the products introduced at that point in the curriculum.Click a Product Hub to center it and view its "Full Route Field" (all multiplication entries) and its "Exits" (division routes).Observe Compression in later stages to help students see how multiple routes can lead to the same product object.
